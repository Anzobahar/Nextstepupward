About us

Coaching that's honest about what it is

NextStepUpward B.V. is a Dutch company offering online lifestyle coaching and mentoring across the Netherlands, Belgium and the wider EU.

NextStepUpward B.V.Registered in the Netherlands · Serving the EU
Our approach

Everyday change, delivered by real people

We focus on the everyday: routines, stress, sleep, and productivity. Our services are non-medical — coaching and mentoring, not therapy or medical advice.

Sessions are delivered digitally by vetted coaches working with us under service contracts, so you always know who you're meeting and what to expect.

  • Merchant of Record. All payments are collected by the Dutch entity, with receipts and invoices.
  • Privacy by design. We store minimal personal data and never keep card details on our servers.
What we value

Principles we run the business on

Non-medical & honest

We're clear about our scope. Coaching and mentoring for lifestyle — never a substitute for medical care.

Transparent & auditable

Receipts for every payment, invoices for every service, and clear booking confirmations.

People first

Vetted coaches, fair policies, and support that treats you like a person — not a ticket number.
