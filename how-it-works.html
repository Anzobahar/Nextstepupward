How it works

From sign-up to your first win in four steps

A clear, low-pressure path. You book, we match you with a coach, and you start making progress — usually within the first week.

Choose a program

Pick a single session, a 5-session package, or a monthly membership that fits your goals and budget. Not sure? Start small.

Check out securely

Pay via Stripe or Mollie — iDEAL, Bancontact, SEPA Direct Debit, or card. Your payment is charged at checkout and a receipt is emailed instantly.

Book your sessions

Open the scheduler and pick times that suit you. Confirmations and reminders arrive by email, so nothing slips.

Meet & grow online

Join your coach on a video call, agree on a plan, and track your habits between sessions. Review progress and adjust as you go.

Between sessions

Progress that doesn't stop when the call ends

Coaching works because of what happens between calls. You'll leave each session with one or two small actions and a simple way to track them — so momentum keeps building.

  • Clear next steps after every session
  • Habit tracking to keep you honest
  • Session history & receipts in your account
Payments & peace of mind

Safe, transparent, and easy to cancel

Secure by design

Payments run through Stripe and Mollie. We never store your card details on our servers.

Receipts & records

Every payment generates a receipt, and your bookings and history live in your account.

Reschedule freely

Life happens. Reschedule with 24 hours' notice, and cancel memberships anytime. See our refund policy.

Ready to take the first step?

Book a single session and see how it feels. Most people leave their first call with a plan they can start that evening.
