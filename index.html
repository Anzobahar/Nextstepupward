About us

Coaching that's honest about what it is

NextStepUpward B.V. is a Dutch company offering online lifestyle coaching and mentoring across the Netherlands, Belgium and the wider EU. We focus on the everyday: routines, stress, sleep, and productivity.

Our services are non-medical — we provide coaching and mentoring, not therapy or medical advice. Sessions are delivered digitally by vetted coaches working with us under service contracts, so you always know who you're meeting and what to expect.

  • Merchant of Record. All payments are collected by the Dutch entity, with receipts and invoices.
  • Privacy by design. We store minimal personal data and never keep card details on our servers.
Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is this medical treatment or therapy?

No. NextStepUpward provides non-medical coaching and mentoring focused on lifestyle and personal development. We do not diagnose, treat, or provide medical or psychological therapy. If you need medical or mental-health care, please contact a licensed professional or your GP.

How are sessions delivered?

All sessions happen online over video call. After booking, you'll receive a confirmation email with your session time and a link to join. You can attend from anywhere in the EU.

How do payments work?

Checkout is handled securely by Stripe and Mollie. You can pay with iDEAL, Bancontact, SEPA Direct Debit, or credit/debit card. Payment is charged at checkout and a receipt is emailed to you automatically. We never store your card data.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes. You can reschedule or cancel through your account under our fair cancellation policy. Please see our Refund & Cancellation Policy for full details and timeframes.

What happens to my data?

We follow GDPR and store only what's needed to deliver the service — typically your name, email, and a payment token. We never keep card numbers on our servers. Read our Privacy Policy for the full picture.

Do you offer memberships and courses?

Yes. Alongside 1-on-1 sessions and packages, our monthly membership includes live sessions, a library of digital courses and workshops, group sessions, and habit-tracking tools.
